Who is Audubon?

Biography

John James Audubon was born in Saint Dominigue (now Haiti) on April 26, 1785. He was the son of Jean Audubon, a French adventurer, and Mademoiselle Rabin, about whom little is known except that she was a Creole and died soon after her son's birth. What is this Exhibition about?

This exhibition is dedicated to the memory of John James Audubon, organized by Meghan Doherty, and is taking place at the Berea College library. The purpose of this exhibition is to show off the artwork and plates of the naturalist, John James Audubon.
