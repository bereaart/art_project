What is this website about?

This website was created as part of my research paper for the History of Printmaking class taught by Meghan Doherty. I chose to write about one of the most well-known American naturalists of all time, John James Audubon. In order to reach a larger audience, I decided to create a website that introduces John James Audubon to others and allows people to grow interest to his work. Moreover, my website showcases exhibition labels from the Berea College library which explores the work of John James Audubon within the context of the history of natural history.
